About Me

My name is Gabriel Vermund. I am an energetic, passionate, reliable person looking for a career in the entertainment business. I have experience working many minimum-wage jobs including in places like Disney World and Regal Entertainment. I am currently pursuing a certificate in web-development from the University of Central Florida through its part-time Bootcamp.

A career in tech interests me in large part because of the growing nature of it. I feel like being a part of this industry or at the very least a basic familiarity with the concepts of web-design and web-development will give me an edge over the competition for all sorts of jobs in the job market.
